LAZARUS

Round - 3 Thematic images

Guidelines for Round 3 of Lazarus
  • Only the shortlisted participants of Round 2 would be eligible to participate in Round 3. Shortlisted participants will receive communication about their selection in Round 1 from our club mail ID music.activity@iiserkol.ac.in. A list of shortlisted participants will also be available on the Instagram handle of Revival as well as the website of Revival.
  • There will be only 1 thematic image in this round. Your musical performance should be based upon this thematic image.
  • Participants should also write a few lines explaining how their music is intertwined with the theme of the images or they should interpret the image in the google form.
  • Participants should display a slide, elucidating their Name, their Institute's name, name of the song, and Image no. in the first 5 secs of their submission video. It is compulsory to show your face and visuals of you performing in the video otherwise your entry will be disqualified.

Best of luck!
