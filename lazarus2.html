    Guidelines for Round 2 of Lazarus

  • Only the shortlisted participants of Round 1 would be eligible to participate in Round 2. Shortlisted participants will recieve communication about their selection in Round 1 from our club mail ID music.activity@iiserkol.ac.in . List of shortlisted participants will also be available on Instagram handle of Revival as well as website of Revival.
  • The theme of this round is the common emotion expressed by all the given images. The participants are expected to interpret and understand the common theme and perform a musical piece based upon it and make a video of your performance.
  • Participants should also write a few lines explaining how their music is intertwined with the theme of the images or they should interpret the image in the google form.
  • Participants should display a slide, elucidating their Name, their Institute's name, name of the song, and Image no. in the first 5 secs of their submission video. It is compulsory to show your face and visualsof you performing in the video otherwise your entry will be disqualified.

Best of luck!
